Privacy Policy

How PrivacyPulse collects, uses, and protects your information in our vigilant pursuit of data security.

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

PrivacyPulse is committed to protecting user privacy and maintaining transparency about how information is collected and used. This policy outlines our vigilant approach to safeguarding your digital footprint while providing premium privacy analysis services.

2. Information We Collect

To provide accurate privacy analysis, we collect the following data points:

  • Website URLs
  • Community submissions
  • Policy URLs
  • Product names
  • Usage analytics
  • Device/browser info

3. How We Use Information

  • Generate comprehensive privacy reports for analyzed services.
  • Improve the accuracy of our privacy scoring algorithms.
  • Process community contributions for database enrichment.
  • Maintain security and prevent malicious use of the platform.
  • Optimize User Experience (UX) through behavioral analysis.

4. Community Contributions

Our platform thrives on community-driven intelligence. Privacy policy submissions undergo review by our security team and are added to our global database to help other security-conscious users.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Your data is shared with third parties only when legally required by authorized entities or to protect the safety and rights of PrivacyPulse and its community.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-leading technical and organizational measures, including end-to-end encryption and regular security audits, to ensure your information remains protected from unauthorized access or disclosure.

7. User Rights

You maintain full control over your data. You have the right to:

Request access to your stored data ›
Correct inaccuracies in your profile ›
Request permanent deletion of your account ›

8. Cookies & Analytics

PrivacyPulse uses essential cookies to maintain your session and aggregate analytics to improve our services. We prioritize privacy-first analytics providers that respect "Do Not Track" signals.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. Significant updates will be reflected by a new "Last Updated" date at the top of this document.

10. Contact Us

If you have any privacy-related concerns or questions about this policy, please reach out to our security team.
