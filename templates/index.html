How PrivacyPulse Scores Websites

Our AI engine evaluates platforms across five core privacy dimensions.

Data Collection

Quantity and sensitivity of data harvested.

Tracking & Cookies

Data Sharing

User Rights

Ability to delete, export, or opt-out.

Transparency

Clarity and readability of policy terms.
